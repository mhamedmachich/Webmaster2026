## Recommended Database Expansion Plan

1. **Add national finders first:** USAGov Benefits, Benefit Finder, HUD Resource Locator, FindTreatment.gov, CareerOneStop, National Literacy Directory, and Findhelp.org.
2. **Add state-specific resources next:** create one dataset per state with food, housing, employment, legal aid, health, student/youth, disability, elder care, childcare, and transportation resources.
3. **Add trust metadata to every entry:** source owner, last verified date, update frequency, official link, phone policy, accessibility notes, language availability, and whether hours were verified.
4. **Add taxonomy fields:** national/state/local scope, eligibility requirements, documentation needed, service area, remote/in-person availability, and emergency disclaimer status.
5. **Add content moderation fields:** public submission status, reviewed by, review notes, duplicate detection key, and stale-data alert date.

## Manual Review Checklist

- Confirm the organization still exists and serves the stated location.
- Confirm service eligibility, cost, languages, and hours.
- Use official source links when possible.
- Avoid adding call-to-action phone buttons for numbers the project intentionally does not want surfaced.
- Store a visible `verifiedDate` and mark stale entries as `needsReview: true`.
